GRADIENTS

I currently don't know how to make this an actual shop, but these are examples of each of the products I have. There are custom gradients that I've created, and each pack contains three gradients that each use a general palette. I also have Actions that can be imported and used within Photoshop to apply different effects to your images. I will try to add examples of what they can do later.


ACID GRADIENT PACK:
The Acid Gradient Pack is a set of gradients to give your images a sickly, corroded look.



FROZEN GRADIENT PACK:
The Frozen Gradient Pack is a set of gradients to give your images a cool, icy look.



THERMAL GRADIENT PACK:
The Thermal gradient Pack is a set of gradients to give your images a full spectrum heat-map look.
