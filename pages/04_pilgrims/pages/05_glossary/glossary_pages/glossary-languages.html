Languages

Many languages are spoken throughout the world. Characters may have trouble communicating with other cultures and will need to either learn the language, find a translator, or figure out ways to communicate without using words.

Konigisch
Spoken in Konigebiet. A language commonly spoken in ports frequented by Konigebietan traders, those who speak Konigisch are bound to find a friend if they have some coin.

Ghasahrun
Spoken in Ghasahra. Traders may speak some Ghasahrun, but aside from herbalists, who consider it surpassingly useful, it is not widely used outside Ghasahra.

Jogug-In
Spoken in Jogug. Closely guarded language, not spoken outside Jogug except by a few trusted traders. Jogug-In's specialty lies in it's breadth of terms for materials and their uses in various stages of industrial production.

Basa Rawa
Spoken in Rawageni. Not often spoken outside Rawageni territory, and considered somewhat primitive by most explorers, Basa Rawa relies on connections to cultural mythology to convey meaning.

Stridsku
Spoken in Stridsland. A harsh language regarded as brutal by the people the Stridlanders raid. Stridsku is guarded not as a precaution by it's speakers against foreign influence, but because they rarely leave survivors.

Veliki
Spoken in Velikaya. Primarily used in scientific documentation, Veliki is the language of discovery and progress; despised by the Terrasantans, and feared by many others.

Santara
Spoken in Terrasanta. Primarily found in religious texts, Santara is regarded in Terrasanta as the language of tradition and devotion, and in many other cultures as a language of malignant curses.
