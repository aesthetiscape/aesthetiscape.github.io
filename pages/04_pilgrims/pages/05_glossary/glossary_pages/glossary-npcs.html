Generating NPCs

Both the GM and players may have reason to generate NPCs. Any character with a stat block is genrated this way.

First, determine the Soul Value of the NPC. For characters generated as a result of a spell or miracle, the Soul Value should be explicit in the effect description. For spells, created by a player (at higher levels), the player should discuss with the GM the Soul Value according to Psyche and Stamina spent. For other characters, Soul Value is up to the GM's discretion.

For NPCs, each Characteristic is equal to the sum of the Soul Value's number of d6, and each Characteristic is rolled seperately. NPCs generally do not have Capabilities, but in the case that they do, they are generated the same way the player character Capabilities are generated.

The following is an example:
Lesser Demon | Soul Value: 5d6
Vitality: 24
Stamina: 19
Psyche: 10
