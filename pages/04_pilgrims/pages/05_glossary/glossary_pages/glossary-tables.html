Divine Encounter

In a Divine Encounter, the party cannot attack the divine being.

A number of d6s are rolled according to the party's total Curse. Any characters with Curse above zero receive damage during these rolls, and once their Curse is reduced to zero, they cease receiving damage from Divine Encounter rolls.

The encounter continues until all players' Curse total is zero. If a character's health reaches zero, whether their Curse is reduced to zero or not, they die and are sent to the Underworld.

All party members experience purgation as a group. If a character's Curse reaches zero before their health, they remain alive, their Curse is considered purged.
