Spells

Requirement
A Character must meet the minimum Capability score to cast this spell.

Cost
A spell's cost lists the Characteristics that must be spent in order to cast it.

Focus: No / Yes / Optional
Focus allows a caster to maintain an ongoing miracle or spell effect while continuing to cast other miracles or spells which do not require focus. If a caster is already using their focus, then they cannot cast another miracle or spell that requires focus. If focus is listed as "optional," then the caster may choose to use their focus to maintain the effect, or to let it be an instantaneous effect. When maintaining a cast miracle or spell, the Stamina cost for subsequent rounds is reduced to 0.

Electromagnetic

Beam

REQUIREMENT:
Intellect Score ≥ 12
COST:
1 Stamina per target, 2 Psyche per 1d6
FOCUS:
Optional

Focus energy to create a targeted destructive laser beam. Deals non-physical damage.

Light

REQUIREMENT:
Intellect Score ≥ 8
COST:
1 Stamina, 1 Psyche
FOCUS:
Optional

Create a temporary light source in the air, illuminating the immediate surrounding area.

Sonic

Scope

REQUIREMENT:
Intellect Score ≥ 7
COST:
1 Stamina per Target, 1 Psyche per Target
FOCUS:
Optional

Perceive minute details using bursts of high-frequency pressurre waves. Allows for easier detection of things that are hidden, as well as weak points.

Decoy

REQUIREMENT:
Intellect Score ≥ 6
COST:
1 Stamina per Target, 1 Psyche per Target
FOCUS:
No

Create a sound to distract or intimidate. Intimidated targets are unable to attack, and distracted targets are less likely to notice you.

Gravity

Float

REQUIREMENT:
Intellect Score ≥ 13
COST:
3 Stamina per Target, 3 Psyche per Target
FOCUS:
Yes

Decrease the weight of a target without affecting its mass. Allows the target to act more quickly, in combat they may perform one additional action.

Heavy

REQUIREMENT:
Intellect Score ≥ 15
COST:
3 Stamina per Target, 3 Psyche per Target
FOCUS:
Yes

Increase the weight of a target without affecting its mass. Forces the target to act more slowly, in combat they may perform one less action.
