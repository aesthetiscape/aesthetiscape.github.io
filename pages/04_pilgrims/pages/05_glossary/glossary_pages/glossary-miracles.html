Miracles

Requirement

A Character must meet the minimum Capability score to cast this miracle.

Cost

A miracle's cost lists the Characteristics that must be spent in order to cast it.

Focus: No / Yes / Optional

Focus allows a caster to maintain an ongoing miracle or spell effect while continuing to cast other miracles or spells which do not require focus. If a caster is already using their focus, then they cannot cast another miracle or spell that requires focus.

If focus is listed as "optional," then the caster may choose to use their focus to maintain the effect, or to let it be an instantaneous effect. When maintaining a cast miracle or spell, the Stamina cost for subsequent rounds is reduced to 0.

Prayer

Heal

REQUIREMENT:
Insight Score ≥ 8
COST:
1 Stamina per Target, 1 Psyche per Target
FOCUS:
Optional

Relieve the wounds and salve the mind of yourself and your allies. Roll Xd6 and distribute the total between any number of characters of your choice as health.

Jinx

REQUIREMENT:
Insight Score ≥ 10
COST:
1 Stamina per Target, 1 Psyche per Target
FOCUS:
Optional

Pray for protection from harm. During the Enemy Turn, reduce the number of damage rolls by the amount of Psyche spent.

Summon

Ghost

REQUIREMENT:
Insight Score ≥ 14
COST:
2 Stamina per 1d6, 2 Psyche per 1d6
FOCUS:
Yes

Temporarily call a spirit from the immaterial world. In combat, the spirit acts with the party, and during the Enemy Turn, they receive damage as though they were a member of the party. When the spirit's health is reduced to 0, they are released from your summoning and return to the Underworld.

Banish

REQUIREMENT:
Insight Score ≥ 16
COST:
2 Stamina per d6, 2 Psyche per d6
FOCUS:
Yes

Temporarily remove a spirit from the material world. Targets contest the roll with Xd3 based on their Soul Value, and if their result is lower than the result for the roll to banish, they are sent immediately to the underworld. Targets banished in this way do not leave souls behind.
