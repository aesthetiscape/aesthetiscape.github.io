Classes

Classes are journeys which characters may make in order to acquire powerful skills. Players are not required to take classes, but some skills are only available through certain Class paths.

Outlander

A traveler with no renown who has little skill and is unfamiliar with their surroundings. All begin their journey as such, with many roads ahead. Becoming an outlander is the starting point for any person who travels on the road.

Prerequisites

Skills: N/A

Equipment: N/A

Squire

A servant who is trained to support others. Many who wish to enter into noble service, or to win renown for their physicality start as squires.

Prerequisites

At least 1 of 3 Skills: Fast Hands, Multilingual, Simple Weaponry

Equipment: Pledge of Service

Knight

A noble protector who defends the weak. Knights most often serve a court or noble, but under some circumstances they may take up their own cause and roam in search of quests.

Prerequisites

At least 2 of 4 Skills: Advanced Weaponry, Favored Weapon, Heavy Armor, Mounted Combatant

Equipment: Favorite Weapon

Knight Errant

A knight who has lost their way. Turned to brigandry, the Knight-Errant strikes fear into the heart of even the most stalwart traveller.

Prerequisites

Deed of Infamy

Gain 1 of 2 Skills

Bloodlust — You gain strength as your fury grows. With each strike you land on an enemy, you become more ferocious. But as you descend into the path of blood, beware the Curse.

Dauntless — In battle, you are a force to be reckoned with, and your honed instincts occasionally power you through what might kill another.

Warrior

A knight who has made a name for themself. Those who travel alongside a warrior rest easy at night and relax their guard at daytime.

Prerequisites

Token of Favor

Gain Skill

Dauntless — In battle, you are a force to be reckoned with, and your honed instincts occasionally power you through what might kill another.

Apprentice

A wizard's aide. Regarded as a respectable position, families of means often vie for positions for their children to study under wizards and mages of repute.

Prerequisites

At Least 2 of 3 Skills: Astrology, Reading, Writing

Equipment: Divination Tools

Wizard

A wizened sorcerer. The best wizards are known to be great problem solvers, and are often sought by travelers embarking on a journey on behalf of a court.

Prerequisites

3 Skills: Divination, Runescript, Sorcery

Equipment: Untried Spell

Warlock

A wizard who has turned to dark power. Though it may lead them to insanity, the warlock pushes at the boundaries of research with little regard for ethics.

Prerequisites

Malformed Signet

Gain 1 of 2 Skills

Welling Deep — Your long contemplation of astral depths has branded your spirit and redirected your consciousness.

Spell-Craft — Your advanced understanding and your hunger for exploration lead you to discover new ways to push the boundaries of the natural order.

Mage

A wizard who has stayed true to legitimate experimentation. Traditionally their focus is the creation of new magics, though it is not unheard of for a Mage to contribute to research in other fields.

Prerequisites

Signet

Gain Skill

Spell-Craft — Your advanced understanding and your hunger for exploration lead you to discover new ways to push the boundaries of the natural order.

Wanderer

A wayward soul. Wanderers spend almost as much time in society as they do outside it, and it often shows in their manner and dress.

Prerequisites

2 Skills: Fast Hands, Herbalism

Equipment: Herbalist's Tools

Sojourner

A figure of middling renown. When traveling alone, they conceal themselves for safety, even the least skilled are capable of becoming nearly imperceptible to untrained senses.

Prerequisites

2 Skills: Simple Weaponry, Obfuscation

Equipment: Tethering Talisman

Recluse

A wanderer who has come to despise others. They are feared as poisoners and calculating mystics who see far and work their influence for their own gain.

Prerequisites

Congealed Blood

Gain 2 Skills

Beyond the Veil — While ethereal, you are able to interact with living things in the physical world. You appear as a ghastly spirit to the living.

Ethereal Veil — Your understanding of the material has led to a deepened understanding of the immaterial, and enabled you to traverse it as you would the physical.

Sage

A wanderer who offers their skills for the good of others. Sages are held in esteem for their skills in herbalism and for their wisdom.

Prerequisites

Jewel Pendant

Gain Skill

Ethereal Veil — Your understanding of the material has led to a deepened understanding of the immaterial, and enabled you to traverse it as you would the physical.

Acolyte

A cleric's servant. Some acolytes join the cloth out of fascination with spirits, while others get involved in their local chapter simply due to their faith.

Prerequisites

At least 2 of 3 Skills: Multilingual, Reading, Writing

Equipment: Holy Tome

Cleric

A stalwart holy person. Held in high esteem as healers and exorcisers, a cleric's calling is to quell the demons that disturb their locale.

Prerequisites

2 Skills: Miracle-Work, Runescript

Equipment: Spirit Bell

Apostate

A cleric whose faith has failed him. While some clerics who lose faith may simply leave their position, others become apostate. These apostates both knowingly and unknowingly encourage and even facilitate demonic activity.

Prerequisites

Burned Tome

Gain 1 of 2 Skills

Primeval Call — Your bell rings out, summoning from the depths of the underworld ancient spirits, your disregard for the Curse beckons ancient and evil things which defy comprehension.

Summoning — Your bell rings out as you read from your tome, calling forth spirits from your holy tales.

Monk

A cleric who has taken further steps in their vows. Many monks operate solitarily, leading peaceful lives presiding as the spiritual advisor for a small region, though itinerant monks are not entirely unusual, and are generally welcome among bands of travelers.

Prerequisites

Prayer Rope

Gain Skill

Summoning — Your bell rings out as you read from your tome, calling forth spirits from your holy tales.
