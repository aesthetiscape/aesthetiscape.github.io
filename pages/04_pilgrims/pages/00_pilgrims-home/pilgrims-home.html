Welcome to Pilgrims, a tabletop roleplaying game.

Like many other TTRPGs, Pilgrims is meant to be played cooperatively between the Game Master and the Party, and focuses on collaborative world-building and story-telling.

This particular game is the result of several years of thinking about what it would look like to create a game that incentivised a non-combative playstyle through a "Curse" mechanic. Actions that harm or debuff other characters, even the evil enemy characters, result in the accrual of Curse, which will build up over time, and if it isn't removed, can eventually lead to the death of that character.

Thanks to Phillip, Ryan, Taylor, Josh, and John.
